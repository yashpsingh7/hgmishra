Career Bio

Hari Govind Mishra is an assistant professor of marketing with the School of Business, Faculty of management at Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University, Katra. His studies include Geophysics (M.Sc. BHU), MBA (MGKP University, U.P. 1990) & PhD (VBSP University, U.P). As a post graduate student at the Banaras Hindu University, he was awarded UGC Scholarship in final year study of M.Sc. (Tech) Geophysics 2000 as well awarded Gold Medal for standing first position in MBA Examination 2002. After receiving his PhD in Management (VBSP University, UP) he joined the Ghanshyam Binani Academy of Management Studies, Worked as a lecturer from Aug. 2005- May 2006. Later on he joined as an Academic Associate from May 2006 to March 2007 at the Indian Institute Of management, Ahmadabad. His areas of research are Advertising and Branding, Retailing, and Customer Loyalty. He is the author of ‘Modern Marketing Management’ (81-7611-282-8, RBSA Publisher, Jaipur, India), ‘International Marketing Management’ (81-7611-283-6, RBSA Publisher, Jaipur, India) and ‘Marketing Management Techniques of Life Insurance Corporation of India (978-81-7611-464-6, RBSA Publisher, Jaipur, India). He has developed cases on Deewan Beverage Godfather, Jammu (2014); Swasthya Vardhak Pharmacy Pvt. Ltd. Uttar Pradesh (2013); Prathista Industries Limited, Andhra Pradesh (2012); Shalimar Cattle Feed, Jammu (2011); Anand Coir Foam Jammu Pvt. Ltd, Nidra, Jammu (2009) and Pahalwan Food Mall, Jammu (2008). He has done various Projects, few of them are “Impact of FDI on Retail Sector in Jammu”, project sponsored by AICTE ; “Does CSR Activities of the Indian Firma Facilitate market development at BOP” project sponsored by IIM Kolkata as an external collaborator, “Organized Retailing in Jammu”, sponsored by U.G.C and “Enterprising Jammu” sponsored by AICTE. At present he is an Assistant Professor at Shri Mata Viashno devi University, Katra, J&K.
